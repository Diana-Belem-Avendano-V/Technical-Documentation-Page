Cybersecurity Specialist

Cybersecurity specialists protect an organization's computer systems and networks from potential cyberattacks, hacking, and data breaches.

They implement security protocols, monitor threats, and respond to incidents to safeguard sensitive information.

Database Administrator

Database administrators manage and organize an organization's databases, ensuring data is stored securely and efficiently.

They monitor database performance, optimize queries, and perform backups to protect against data loss.
